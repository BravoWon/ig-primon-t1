                  regime      mean KL  mean flip
                 R0 bf16    1.500e-02      9.5%
      R1 weight-only FP8    2.066e-02      9.4%
        R2 full W8A8 FP8    2.824e-02     13.0%

[VERDICT -- what the weight-only sim measured vs what deployment actually costs]
  (A) bf16 ACTIVATION floor (R0=1.50e-02) vs the CPU sim's bf16 weights-only (1.7e-05):
      ~877x larger. The sim held activations at float64, so it never saw the
      activation/residual-stream precision cost -- which DOMINATES real error.
  (B) weight-FP8 MARGINAL on metal (R1-R0=5.66e-03) vs the CPU sim's weight-only FP8 (5.2e-03):
      ~1.1x  -> the sim was FAITHFUL on its own object (weight quant); that object is just SUB-DOMINANT.
  (C) activation-FP8 marginal (R2-R1=7.58e-03): the W8A8 step the weight-only sim could not model.

  => HARDWARE VERDICT: the weight-only precision-allocation arc optimized a ~5e-3 term while the
     dominant ~1.5e-2 deployment error lives in ACTIVATION precision, which weight allocation cannot touch.
     The sim's number is real but second-order. This is the gap the F-app null pointed at, now measured.
